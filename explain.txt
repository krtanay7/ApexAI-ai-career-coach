What is the Project?
AI Career Coach is an intelligent web application designed to help students and professionals prepare for their job search journey. It's like having a personal career advisor available 24/7 who helps you:

Build Professional Resumes - Create impactful resumes with AI-powered suggestions
Practice for Interviews - Take AI-generated mock interview quizzes based on your industry
Generate Cover Letters - Create tailored cover letters for specific job applications
Understand Career Markets - Get real-time industry insights including salary ranges, trending skills, and job demand
Think of it as a complete career preparation toolkit powered by AI that guides students from resume writing to interview preparation.
